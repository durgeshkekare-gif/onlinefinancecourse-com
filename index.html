Other Finance Qualifications & Certifications in India

These qualifications are available through their official bodies. No enrollment support available — listed for reference only.

CFA Institute (USA)
CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst)
Global gold standard for investment professionals · 3 levels · Self-paced exam-based · No enrollment partner · Most valued for equity research, portfolio management, investment banking
ICAI (Institute of Chartered Accountants of India)
CA (Chartered Accountant)
India's most respected finance qualification · 3-level exam-based · 3 years articleship · No entrance exam for CPT · Best for audit, taxation, corporate finance
ICMAI India
CMA (Cost & Management Accountant)
Cost and management accounting · Less brand value than CA but good for manufacturing/industry sector · Exam-based
GARP (Global Association of Risk Professionals)
FRM (Financial Risk Manager)
Global risk management credential · 2-part exam · Most valued for risk management roles at banks and asset management firms
FPSB India
CFP (Certified Financial Planner)
Personal finance and wealth management credential · Exam-based · Growing demand in wealth management sector
